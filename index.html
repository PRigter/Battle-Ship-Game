Welcome to my Battleship Game!

Find out where are the 3 Battleships.
Each Battleship are made of 3 cells. They can be horizontal or vertical diplayed.

You have 4 minutes to find all of them.
Each MISS will decrease time in 5 seconds.
Each HIT will increase time in 5 seconds.
